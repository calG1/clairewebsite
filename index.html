About Claire

Claire Gilbertson

#29

Position: 1B, OF

Grad Year: 2027

Height: 5'11"

Claire Gilbertson is a dedicated and passionate softball player aiming to compete at the collegiate level. Born on June 19, 2009, and graduating in 2027, she is entering her junior year of high school in the fall of 2025.

Standing at 5'11", Claire primarily plays First Base, bringing a strong defensive presence to the infield. She also excels as a Corner Outfielder, showcasing a powerful throwing arm.

Her journey in softball began at age 7, and she has been playing competitive softball since age 10, continually developing her skills and game IQ.

On offense, Claire is primarily a line drive gap-to-gap hitter. She is actively working on her power swing to increase slugging potential and is also focused on improving her agility for enhanced performance on both sides of the ball.

Academically, Claire is interested in Science, Biology, and Animals. Off the field, she enjoys fishing, hunting, reading, swimming, and spending quality time with friends and family.

Claire is committed to her growth as a player and is eager to contribute her talent and hard work to a college softball program.
